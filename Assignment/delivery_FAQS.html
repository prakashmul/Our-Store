Delivery FAQS

Our aim is to make the delivery process smooth and pleasant experience for you. Your orders will be delivered by our best courier partners in Kathmandu and Pokhara. We deliver all 365 days in a year as we put customer satisfaction over anyting else. We have tried to answer some of the commonly asked questions. If you have more doubtts, please feel free to write us at ourstore2022@gmail.com

When will i receive the order?

As soon as we receive your order. We send the products in 1-2 days time with the best courier partner and expected to deliver in 2-3 days based on your location.


How Can we pay?

Our customer can pay for their order via credit card/Debit card/Cash on delivery/Net banking/Digital Wallet. You can pay at the the time of delivery for COD. More online payment options wil be added soon for your convenience.


Which are the delivery areas in Kathmandu?

We deliver in almost every area across Kathmandu and Pokhara.


Which are the delivery areas except Kathmandu and Pokhara?

For now the delivery is done only in Kathmandu and Pokhara but we are thinking of extending our delivery areas and deliver our products in different main cities of Nepal where transportation facilities are available.

Which is the delivery charge?

We offer free home delivery on all orders more than RS.5000 and for order less than RS.5000 we collect RS.100 for delivery.


How my ordered items will be delivered to me?

Our delivery partner will deliver your order upto where the transportation facilities are availale near your home.
